# Demo University Exam Regulations

1. Registration for Exams
Students must register for their exams through the central examination portal no later than 4 weeks before the examination period begins. Late registrations are only accepted under exceptional circumstances and require approval from the examination board.

2. Absence and Illness
If a student is unable to attend an exam due to illness, a medical certificate must be submitted to the examination office within 3 working days of the exam date. Failure to provide this will result in a failed attempt.

3. Academic Integrity
Any form of cheating, including plagiarism or the use of unauthorized materials during an exam, will result in an immediate fail for the course. Serious offenses may lead to expulsion from the university.

4. Retakes
Students are allowed a maximum of two retakes for any failed exam. The first retake typically occurs in the following semester. If the second retake is also failed, the student will not be allowed to continue in their current degree program.
